[b]HUGE DISCLAIMER: I tested it and found no measurable negative impact but it may reduce performance. 
I am touching pathfinding/chores stuff here, let me know in the comments if it cripples your game.[/b]

Prevents Cooking Stations, Microbe Mushers and Fish Feeders from using mutated seeds - until you allow them to do that with a new button. Unidentified seeds are always denied.
Will not remove seeds from stations, only prevents new seeds from being added.
